q To help prevent the spread of COVID-19: Wash your hands frequently. Use soap and water or an alcohol-based solution to sanitize your hands. Keep a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing. Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible. Do not touch your eyes, nose or mouth. Cover your nose and mouth with your elbow or a tissue when coughing or sneezing. Stay home if you feel sick. Seek medical attention if you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing. Your health care provider should be contacted before proceeding so that they can quickly direct you to the appropriate care facility.
